HAIL CAR

HAIL Car are ride-sharing and request-for-a-ride mode of transportation where customers book a ride via their mobile apps. Customers inputs their pick-up and drop-off locations on the request section of the application. After submitting their request, the application will search for an available driver/car nearby which can accommodate their request. The difference with these two from a taxi is the way of acquiring the ride. These two are acquired via mobile app request which only take less than a minute and will fetch you on your pick-up location! While taxi are to be hailed from the streets in order to get a ride.
