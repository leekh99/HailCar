You deserve a safe transportation platform.

Safe enough for us to trust our own mothers, sisters, and daughters to use.
So we implemented these measures:

- Screening

Every driver and vehicle are screened upon registration
and must maintain a star rating above a set minimum.

- Training & Code of Conduct

We continually improve our training system and
Code of Conduct to ensure a high level of service.

- Safety Features

We’re always innovating for your safety.
In fact, we introduced the first “Share Your Ride” feature in the industry.

- Insurance & 24/7 Support

We’ve got you covered with additional Personal Accident
coverage, 24/7 call centres, and emergency response teams


Everyone should have Access to Transportation



Because to us, transportation is a right, not a privilege.

regardless of income, age, or special needs:

Choices

Anywhere Anytime
