Frequently Asked Questions[FAQ]

What is JustShare?

JustHail is a service with a combined fleet of taxis and cars at affordable upfront fixed prices


As a passenger, how do I book JustHail?

Easy! open the vehicle menu in your Hail app and tap on JustHail. With one touch,you can book a ride and the nearest vehicle will arrive shortly


How are vehicles allocated with JustHail?

Hail will automatically select the nearest vehicle for you, either a car or a taxi. Our promise to you, is that you’ll be on your way faster whenever you book a JustHail!


Do I pay metered fees with JustHail's taxis?

All cars and taxis booked via JustGrab will charged a fixed fare! If you prefer metered fare rides, please book a GrabTaxi in the vehicle menu.


I got a taxi via JustGrab. Why did the taxi driver turn on the meter anyway?

Taxi drivers have to turn on the meter whenever there are passengers in the car as this is the law. However rest assured that you’ll only need to pay the amount shown in the Grab app.
