FAQs

I have some difficulty signing in to HailFood. What should I do?

If you are experiencing issues with signing in, please ensure that you have installed the latest version of the Hail and HailFood apps.



How does HailFood work?

HailFood is a food delivery app which we'll deliver your favourite meal to your place to make your life a little bit better.



How do I make account?

After you installed HailFood app, you can press on the 'Sign Up' button and fill in the form of your information such as name, telephone, address and etc. to let us know more about you. We also will only call on you once when we have arrived at your place to deliver your food without calling you again for your information anymore.



What if a restaurant is out of an item?

If a restaurant does run out of an item from time to time after you place and order, you can let us know your preference in this situation including:
1. Contact Me
2. Cancel The Order
